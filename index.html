About Our Company

A Legacy of Trust and Professionalism

SASA SECURITY SERVICES SDN BHD was incorporated in September 1990 as a private and professional Security Organization in Malaysia. Initially established in Kuala Terengganu, our headquarters moved to Kuala Lumpur in 1992, marking the beginning of our gradual and steady expansion.

Today, we proudly operate nationwide, with branches in every state. Our management staff are fully trained in security and investigative duties, bringing essential experience and expertise to our service-oriented organization. We are committed to enhancing the support mechanism that our clients rely on for their peace of mind.
